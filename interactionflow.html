Use case:

Title: Signing in and Joining meeting

Description: Greg wants to sign in to his zoom account and join a meeting his professor has created.

Name of user and role: Greg is a sophmore in college. All of his classes are now being held on Zoom.

Usage preconditions: Greg already has an account on Zoom.

Usage postconditions: Greg is in his Zoom meeting and can see his professor and classmates on his screen.

    Interaction Flow:

  • 1. Greg enters his username and password into the Zoom sign in page.
  • 2. Zoom takes that information and loads Gregs profile home page
  • 3. Greg clicks a join meeting button
  • 4. Zoom loads a prompt the asks Greg to enter a meeting ID
  • 5. Greg enters to ID his professor provide him
  • 6. Zoom loads up the meeting for the ID Greg entered
  • 7. Greg is in his classes meeting

Frequency of use: Every Weekday (everyday he has classes)
